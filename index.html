13 Phosphorescence of Natural Divinity

I love the sight of cloth blowing in the wind. I am looking for something close to this feeling in the appearance of nature. I wander through a sea of motifs in search of the origin of the form. Silhouettes accumulated since the Edo period, militaristic silhouettes of the Showa period, and various motifs wandering among them, disappear and arise, are born and die. The creation of these motifs echoes like the sound of a bell, and the repeated ups and downs of life create the waves. The behavior of fabric blowing in the wind, the vibrations of sound shaking the air, and the slight swaying of light reflections all seem to be connected to unified wave field. The reincarnation of the digital world, and the photons of images that arise and disappear, replay memories scattered in time and space, and hide themselves in the wind and dust. The reverberations of the concrete, the rusty texture of the steel frame, the movement of the bones and muscles, the reverberations of the machine. I want to capture the phosphorescence that is generated at the end of life, the moment when faith is born in nature. I wonder if the inner, natural faith that arises here is the spirituality that is contained in the environment in which I grew up. I want to capture this phosphorescent spirituality inside the frame.

13 燐光する霊性

風に吹かれる布の姿が好きだ．自然の見せる姿の中に，この感覚に近しいものを探している．姿形のオリジンを探して，さまざまなモチーフの海をさまよう．江戸から蓄積されたシルエットや昭和の軍国的なシルエット，その中に彷徨うさまざまなモチーフが消えては生じて，生まれては死んでいく．その生成は鈴の音のようにこだまし，生命的な起伏を繰り返しては１つの波を作る．風に吹かれる布の振る舞いも，空気を揺らす音の振動も，光の反射の作るわずかな揺れも全ては１つの波の場に繋がっているようだ．デジタルの世界の輪廻転生も，生じては消える映像のフォトンも，時空間の中に点在する記憶を再生し，風塵の中に姿を隠す．コンクリートの持つ反響，鉄骨の持つ錆びた手触り，骨と筋肉の運動，軀の残響．生命の終わりに描かれる燐光が，自然の中で信仰を生み出すような瞬間を切り取りたい．ここに生じる内発的で，自然の内に秘めた信仰は，僕が育ってきた環境に内包される霊性だろうか．その燐光する霊性をフレームの内側に切り出す。

14 Scattered Focus Window

In the course of researching and exploring media and artworks, I have come to see the longing for a world that is framed in a various way within a diverse range of frames. I am looking at the difference between analog and digital through a window of disparate perspectives.
